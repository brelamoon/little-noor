Little Noor Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Scope

This policy explains how the Little Noor app handles data. The app is primarily offline in the current stage and does not require account registration.

2. Data We Process

Local on-device data: mission progress, settings, rewards, and parent PIN.

Permissions (optional): location is used for prayer times/Qibla; notifications are used for local reminders.

Payments: subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store. We do not store full card data.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • No mandatory account registration in this stage.
  • No custom advertising SDK data collection.
  • No sale of personal data.

4. Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on the device.

5. Children and Parent Controls

The app is designed for family use with parent supervision. Parent settings are protected by PIN.

6. Retention and Deletion

Most data is stored locally. You can reset progress inside the app. Deleting the app removes local app data from the device.

7. Contact

Privacy: privacy@little-noor.app

Support: https://t.me/little_noor_support
