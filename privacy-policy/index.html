Little Noor Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Scope

This policy explains how the Little Noor app handles data. The app is primarily offline in the current stage and does not require account registration.

2. Data We Process

Local on-device data: mission progress, good deeds, settings, rewards, and access PIN.

Storage: all data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Permissions (optional): location is used for prayer times/Qibla; notifications are used for local reminders.

Location privacy: location data is not stored or linked to the user’s identity.

Payments: subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store. We do not store full card data.

3. Advertising and Promotions

The app may display internal promotions related to our products or services. No third-party advertising networks are used.

4. What We Do Not Collect

  • No mandatory account registration in this stage.
  • No sale of personal data.

5. Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on the device.

6. Children and Parent Controls

The app is designed for family use with parent supervision. Parent settings can be protected by PIN.

7. Retention and Deletion

Most data is stored locally. You can reset progress inside the app. Deleting the app removes local app data from the device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact
