# Little Noor Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Little Noor mobile app handles data.

## 1. Scope

Little Noor is an educational app for families. The first stage of the app is primarily offline and does not require account registration.

## 2. Data We Process

### 2.1 Data stored locally on your device

- Learning progress and rewards (missions, XP, streaks, collection state).
- App settings (language, theme, notification preference, prayer method preferences, child profile selection).
- Parent PIN (stored locally for parental access).

### 2.2 Device permissions

- Location permission (if granted) is used to calculate prayer times and Qibla accurately.
- Notification permission (if granted) is used for local reminder notifications.

### 2.3 Subscription and payment data

- Paid subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store (StoreKit / App Store Connect).
- We do not receive or store full card numbers.
- Apple may provide transaction/subscription status data needed to unlock premium access.

## 3. What We Do Not Collect in This Stage

- No user account registration data.
- No cloud profile backend data.
- No custom advertising identifiers for ads.
- No sale of personal data.

## 4. Notifications

- Notifications are local reminders scheduled on the device.
- We do not use our own remote push server in the current stage.

## 5. Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal information.
- We do not share user profiles with data brokers.
- Data may be processed by platform providers only where needed to run app features (for example, Apple for subscriptions and system services).

## 6. Children and Parents

- The app is designed for family use with parent supervision.
- Parent section is protected by a PIN.
- Parents can reset local progress inside the app.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

- Most data is kept locally on the device.
- You can reset progress in settings.
- Deleting the app removes locally stored app data from the device.

## 8. International Users

App distribution may involve Apple infrastructure in different regions according to Apple policies.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will show the latest revision.
